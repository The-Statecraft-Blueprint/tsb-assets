Something Changed in 1995

Party-line voting % (red, right axis) shown annually 1969–2025. Incumbency rate (blue, left axis) shown for election years only — plotted at the year the new Congress was seated (odd years) for alignment with party-line voting.

Methodology note: Incumbency is measured in even years (elections) but the effect shows in odd years (when new members are seated and voting). This view plots incumbency at the odd year following each election to align the two series properly. Wave elections marked in grey.
